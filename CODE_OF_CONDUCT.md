# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful and considerate
- Welcoming different viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.

Report issues to: imaginarium@voyagersha.com
